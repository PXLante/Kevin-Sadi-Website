More About Me

My family is originally from Afghanistan and Iran. I am part of the Baha'i faith. We believe in the unity of mankind. However, in Iran, the Baha'i religion is not tolerated. Because of this, both my mother and father had to escape from Iran. My mom's family moved to Italy, however, my dad struggled to escape because he had nothing in Iran. After he spent time jumping from one country to another, he met and married my mother in Italy. They then moved to America where I was born.


I was born in Atlanta, Georgia. However, I moved to Italy with my family soon after for a year. My family decided that America would be better for me to get an education. Now I have no family in Iran, where my family on my mother's side is in Italy. I am incredibly grateful to be in America today, for the opportunity it has given me to obtain an education and celebrate my culture. I hope to meet new people along my journey and give back to America for all it has given to me.
